Drop Days Supply Calculator

This tab allows you to quickly calculate the days supply for eye and ear drops.

This works well for when the days supply does not auto populate and when verifying that days supply was calculated correctly.

Put in the total quantity, the drops per dose, and the amount of doses per day, as well as if it is the right, left, or both eyes/ears.

Press calculate to see the calculated days supply.
