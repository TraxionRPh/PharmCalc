Accumulation Calculator

This tab allows you to find how much of a medication a patient has accumulated based on given previous fill dates and days supply dispensed.

This works well for seeing if a patient has filled enough sequential fills early that they have amassed a stockpile.

Click "Add Entry" to add as many fill dates needed and input the fill dates and days supply dispensed.

The entries do not need to be in date-order.

Press calculate to see the amount accumulated.
